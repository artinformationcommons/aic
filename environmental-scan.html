Environmental Scan

May 2019 - Ongoing
The Environmental Scan is an ongoing survey of the information, projects, people, tools, and resources that reflect explorations and innovations throughout the cultural heritage community as well as in other commercial sectors. We use the environmental scan to learn lessons that have been documented elsewhere, keep an eye on metadata/technology standards initiatives, identify technologies that we may want to try out or assess in our next steps, and communicate with other projects that are pursuing useful outcomes. You may view a white paper on this environmental scan here and the working document on which this paper is based here.
